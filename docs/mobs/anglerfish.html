Abilities

Spawning fish with glowstone

When you feed one glowstone powder or a glowstone block, it will appear random fish from whatever biome it's in for some seconds, as well as attract some of them.

The more water around the boosted anglerfish, the more fish it will appear.

Drop

Players can also get an "Anglerfish Light" by killing an anglerfish, the anglerfish light makes small fish follow you, wild mercats not fear you, and can also be put on the offhand.

Glowstone blocks last 4 times as much as glowstone dust.
