Taming

They tamed using sea pizza, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them.

Abilities

Turning fish to nuggets

Electric eels randomly electrocute mobs near them, more frequently Once tamed.

When a cod, a salmon, a tropical fish or a mob from this add-on is generally killed by an electric eel it drops random items, mostly ore nuggets, including diamond nuggets. Also, tamed mobs from this add-on are generally immune to the shock.
