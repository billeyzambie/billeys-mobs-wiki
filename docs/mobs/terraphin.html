Taming

Abilities

Useage

They are getting a less confusing and more useful use soon but currently, Once tamed, they defend their owner, Players can ride them, though to control them you need to craft terraphin staff(fishing rod, happy powder, and either raw withering snail, cooked regeneration snail or a poultry orange).

Players can boost them while riding them with orange peels, chocolate catfish or terraphin staff. While boosted, they have a lot more speed, health and attack damage. When the boost finishes they drop a terraphin furball which can be thrown to give levitation. They also kinda help you find diamond ores and mob spawners but I'm not sure if it works. A terraphin's variant depends on the biome it spawned in, terraphins appear in any kind of forest, including snowed forests and flower forests Try breeding 2 ones of a different variant.
