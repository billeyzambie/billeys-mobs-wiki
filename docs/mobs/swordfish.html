In-game info book text

=========SWORDFISH=========

Swordfish spawn in oceans, can be put in a bucket and tamed with sea pizza, but they don't follow you unless you use a stick on them. They currently don't have a use while they are alive but when they die they drop several body parts as well as raw meat. The body parts can be used to craft a fish sword which does a lot of damage to fish and can kill multiple fish at once.
