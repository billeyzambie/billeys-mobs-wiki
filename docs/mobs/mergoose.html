Mergeese appear in lakes and rivers.

Taming

They are tamed using sea pizza, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them.

Abilities

Mergoose Spikes

When they attack something they drop mergoose spikes, which can be used on stick bugs to make them deal twice as much attack damage temporalily.

This ability has no extra conditions beyond the usage described above.

Mergoose Sword

Mergoose spikes can also be used to craft a mergoose sword(diamond sword + 8 mergoose spikes), which is generally like a normal iron sword but it can deal damage from quite a long distance if you press the button you press to throw items(eg.

long tap on mobile, right click on pc). And also Once tamed mergeese defend their owner.

In-game info book text

=========MERGOOSE=========

- General
Mergeese spawn in lakes and rivers and are tamed with sea pizza, but they don't follow you unless you use a stick on them. They have to breathe air and can survive indefinetly on land, but they'll try crawl back into water. They also have a long spiky tongue which they use to stab targets from a quite long distance, their target will almost always not land a single hit on them, even if it's a player, unless it has a ranged attack or a ranged weapon.

- Mergoose Spikes
When they attack something they drop mergoose spikes, which can be used on stick bugs to make them deal twice as much attack damage temporalily

- Mergoose Sword
Mergoose spikes can also be used to craft a mergoose sword(diamond sword + 8 mergoose spikes), which is like a normal iron sword but it can deal damage from quite a long distance if you press the button you press to throw items(eg. long tap on mobile, right click on pc). And also after being tamed mergeese defend their owner.
