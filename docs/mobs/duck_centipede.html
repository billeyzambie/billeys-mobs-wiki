=========DUCK CENTIPEDE (BOSS)=========

When a duck kills a velvet worm you have a chance to get a Duck Centipede boss spawn egg which you shouldn't spawn in your house nor right outside. Duck centipedes drop throwable ducks than can be thrown and attack everything other than other ducks, tamed mobs and the player who threw them. Thrown ducks can also be picked up.
