Abilities

Velvet Slime Balls

Velvet worms destroy cobwebs, and attack most wild arthropods(eg.

spiders, silverfish, wild pill bugs and stick bugs) and most of the time when they attack something, they drop velvet slime balls, which can be thrown or crafted into velvet slime blocks.

Velvet Slime Blocks

Purple-green velvet slime blocks give withering and slowness to monsters that walk over them, blue-green ones give speed to players and mixed ones give regeneration and less speed to players.

-Duck Centipede (Boss) When a duck kills one you have a chance to get a Duck Centipede boss appear egg which you shouldn't appear in your house nor right outside. Duck centipedes drop throwable ducks than can be thrown and attack everything other than other ducks, tamed mobs and the player who threw them. Thrown ducks can also be picked up.
