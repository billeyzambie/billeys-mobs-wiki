Piranha

Piranhas appear in lakes and rivers, can be scooped into a bucket and tamed using sea pizza, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them. When they die they drop a raw piranha and sometimes a random water related item.

Obtaining / Spawning

Piranhas appear in lakes and rivers, can be scooped into a bucket.

Taming

They tamed using sea pizza, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them.

Abilities

Piranha Launcher

Raw piranha can be used, along with 3 iron ingots and a piece of redstone powder, to craft a piranha launcher.

To pick up piranhas, hit them while holding a piranha launcher, a piranha launcher can be loaded with up to 64 piranhas.
