=========PICKLEPET=========

Picklepets spawn in coral reefs and are tamed with several sea items. They don't really have an actual use aside from looking funny and being able to be milked with a bottle for pickle juice, which can be drunk or used to craft the colorful potion, which can be used on tamed mobs. But you can also use a pickle cat or a cucumbeel for that. They are getting a unique use soon.
