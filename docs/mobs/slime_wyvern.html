Obtaining / Spawning

Slime wyverns appear in swamps.

Taming

They are tamed using slime balls or slime blocks.

Abilities

Putting on your head for slow falling

After taming one, you'll be able to put it on your head by petting it(sneak + right click/long tap).

Having a slime wyvern on your head gives you slow falling and slowness I.

Other

They Tamed slime wyverns also defend their owner but they will also attack phantoms even if they aren't commanded to.

This ability has no extra conditions beyond the usage described above.

Leveling

Slime wyverns can level up by killing mobs, when they level up their health and attack damage increase.

Mobs that level up by killing things progress through leveling more by hitting mobs than by just killing, which means that to level up your mob faster, make it kill mobs with lots of health.

The default level is generally level 1 and the max is level 10.

On level 10 slime wyverns stop giving you slowness when on your head and instead give only slow falling.

Hits grant 0.6 XP and kills grant 1 XP before multipliers.

Anniversary pet hats double XP, and happiness multiplies XP gains by (1 + effective happiness).
