Obtaining / Spawning

Pizzafish appear in coral reefs, can be scooped into a bucket.

Taming

They tamed using rotten flesh, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them.

Abilities

Sea Pizza

Players can either kill them to get sea pizza or just shear them to get sea pizza, they will eventually regrow their pieces.

Sea pizza is quite nutritious and has a chance to give you a lot of regeneration for a few seconds, and it can also be used to tame fish from the add-on, and vanilla fish, so that they will not despawn and will not attack eachother. Tamed fish can also be healed and leashed.
