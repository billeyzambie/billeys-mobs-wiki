Abilities

Pet Hat

The pet hat makes a mob absorb any kind of damage once and then the hat gets destroyed, and to take it off try putting a hat on a mob that's wearing one.

The pet hat, leather duck armor and end rod duck armor are dyeable by dyeing the mob wearing it. The pet hat can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

In-game info book text

=========KIWI=========

- General
Kiwis spawn in forests during the night and are tamed with spider eyes. If you kill or shear one you get kiwi feathers. With it you can craft equipment for your pets, specifically duck armor and the pet hat. Sheared kiwis can also be bone mealed.

- Pet Hat
The pet hat makes a mob absorb any kind of damage once and then the hat gets destroyed, and to take it off try putting a hat on a mob that's wearing one. The pet hat, leather duck armor and end rod duck armor are dyeable by dyeing the mob wearing it. The pet hat can be put on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.
