=========ANGEL PIG=========

Angel pigs spawn in flower forests and are tamed with golden carrots. After being tamed, they randomly give resistance to nearby tamed mobs from billey's mobs, as well as vanilla fish, cats and parrots. They also randomly give levitation and withering to nearby monsters.
