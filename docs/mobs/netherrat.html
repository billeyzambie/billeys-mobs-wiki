Obtaining / Spawning

Netherrats appear in nether biomes other than the old/default/plain one.

Taming

They are tamed using crimson fungus or warped fungus.

Abilities

Robbing Mobs

After taming them, you'll be able to make them attack and rob villagers, wandering traders and piglins making them drop emerald and gold, or some of their trades.

However, unlike overworld rats, netherrats can defend their owner from any kind of mob whether they can rob it or not, but they're still quite weak.

Cooking

If you equip a netherrat with a pet chef hat(crafted with kiwi feathers and wool) and give it a bowl, you'll get a bowl of spicy ratatouille, which gives regeneration, resistance and fire resistance when eaten but makes you loose a bit of health.

If you give it a glass bottle instead, you'll get a bottle of warped wine, which gives haste when drank but also has a chance to give nausea and/or weakness.

Putting them on your head

Players can also put them on your head by petting them(sneak + right click/long tap) and then walking into them.

Netherrats will usually get off your head either when you jump while sneaking or when you hit a mob netherrats can rob. Also, while netherrats are generally on top of your head, they are immune to any kind of damage.

Breeding

Crossbreeding

Players can get color patterns by breeding two netherrats of a different color.
