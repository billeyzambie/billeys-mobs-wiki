In-game info book text

=========ENDERCAT=========

Endercats spawn in the End and are tamed with chorus fruit. After being tamed they no longer attack vanilla cats, randomly teleport monsters away, and if you sleep while one is not sitting, you'll get random end items when you wake up. You can also feed them a popped chorus fruit to teleport the nearest monster away but it has a cooldown of 3 seconds.
