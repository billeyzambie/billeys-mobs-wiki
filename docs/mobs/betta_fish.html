In-game info book text

=========BETTA FISH=========

Betta fish spawn rarely in lakes and rivers, can be put in a bucket and tamed with sea pizza, but they don't follow you unless you use a stick on them. They currently don't really have any use while they are alive(I'm open to suggestions), but when they die they drop a raw betta fish which can be eaten raw to give you levitation or cooked to give quite some nutrition and saturation. You can also breed them to get secret variants, you get hints when you complete advancements, but you can also just randomly breed different variants and you'll probably get something new.
