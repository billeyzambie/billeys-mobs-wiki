Obtaining / Spawning

Catfish appear in lakes and rivers, can be scooped into a bucket.

Taming

They tamed using sea pizza or seeds, but they do not follow you unless you use a stick on them.

Abilities

Chocolate Catfish

When they die they drop a raw catfish which can be eaten either cooked or raw to get string or used with cocoa beans in a crafting table and then cooked to get a chocolate catfish which gives a lot of speed for quite some time.

Chocolate catfish can also be used to boost terraphins and cucumbeels while riding them for an entire minute.

Catfish Antibodies

Players can get temporary immunity to a particular effect by injecting yourself with catfish antibodies while you have the effects you want to get immunity from.

The immunity lasts for 10 minutes, and also getting a new immunity clears previous immunities.Catfish antibodies can also be injected in mobs, tamed or not, to do the same thing. Catfish antibodies can also make you immune to beneficial effects so beware of that unless you're injecting a hostile mob that gives itself beneficial effects. Additionally the injections deals 1 HP or half a heart of damage but cannot be used to kill things other than killing yourself.

Obtaining Catfish Antibodies

First you have to craft a Syringe with 1 kiwi fur, 1 glass and 1 diamond.

Then tame a catfish and use the syringe on it to get Catfish Blood, the catfish will lose 3 HP or 1.5 hearts of its health. Players can then brew the catfish blood with nether wart to get Catfish Antibodies.
