Obtaining / Spawning

Pill bugs(also known as woodlice and rolly pollys) appear in underground in most biomes.

Taming

They are tamed using poultry oranges, apples, melon or sweet berries.

Abilities

Speeding Up Crop Growth

Once tamed, they speed up vanilla crop growth, I recommend putting some in a fenced crop farm and making them wander around with a stick.

This ability has no extra conditions beyond the usage described above.

Turning dirt to grass

Pill bugs also randomly turn the dirt block under them into a grass block.

This ability has no extra conditions beyond the usage described above.

Breeding

Crossbreeding

Most of the time when you breed 2 ones of a different variant you'll probably get a new non-naturally spawning variant(not all possible combinations result in another variant) that instead of speeding up crop growth, turns full grown wheat into a block that drops a random food item from the add-on and a single wheat seed.

Players can also boost pill bugs with pigeon poop.
