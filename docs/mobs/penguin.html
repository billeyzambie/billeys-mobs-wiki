Obtaining / Spawning

Penguins appear in beaches and frozen oceans.

They are tamed using sea pizza(obtained by pizzafish that appear in warm oceans).

Taming

They are tamed using sea pizza(obtained by pizzafish that appear in warm oceans).

Abilities

Ice Cream

If you put a pet chef hat(crafted with kiwi feathers and wool) on a penguin and give it sugar, it will make you an ice cream, ice cream gives you a few beneficial effects, and is even more powerful in warm biomes and in the nether.

However in cold biomes it gives you detrimental effects instead.

Penguin Boots

Penguins also randomly shed feathers with which Players can craft penguin boots which give you lots of speed on snow and ice, especially on ice, and even more on packed and blue ice, as well as a little bit of speed on sand.

This ability has no extra conditions beyond the usage described above.
