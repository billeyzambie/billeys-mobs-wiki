In-game info book text

=========PICKLE CAT=========

- General
Pickle cats spawn in coral reefs and are tamed with raw cod, raw salmon, sea pizza or raw catfish. After being tamed, they randomly give water breathing to nearby tamed mobs from billey's mobs, as well as vanilla fish, cats and parrots. They can also be milked with a bottle to get pickle juice to drink or to craft the colorful potion. You can also dye them.
