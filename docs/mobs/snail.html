In-game info book text

=========SNAIL=========

- General
Snails spawn in most biomes(same as pigs and cows) and are tamed with apples, leaves ,flowers, sea pickles and many other items. There are 3 types of snail, each one gives a certain potion effect for a really long time, ones with straight antennas give weakness, ones with slightly tilted antennas give slowness and ones with quite tilted antennas give withering. They are mostly useful when you kill and cook them as they give the effect opposite of what they gave when they were alive after being cooked and can also be used to heal any mob in the add-on, but you can also tame them and have them defend you, and also angel mobs give them a lot of buffs. Keep in mind that moving them around is quite hard since they can't teleport to their owner and are very slow.
