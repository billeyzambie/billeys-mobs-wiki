Obtaining / Spawning

Orange penguins appear in badlands.

Taming

They are tamed using tropical fish or raw betta fish.

Abilities

Poultry Oranges

Once tamed, oranges will randomly grow on their head, punch them(they will not get mad do not worry) or shear them to take it, also, feed the orange penguin fish or have an angel cat nearby to speed up the orange's growth.

Poultry oranges can be eaten, and are generally slightly better than cooked fish. When you eat a poultry orange you get an orange peel, which can be used to boost cucumbeels and terraphins while riding them. Poultry oranges can also be used to breed or heal any mob in the add-on.
