In-game info book text

=========CUCUMBEEL=========

- General
Cucumbeels spawn in coral reefs and are tamed with sea pizza, but they don't follow you unless you use a stick on them. After being tamed they can be ridden underwater and will give you 5 seconds of water breathing whenever you jump out of water with one. They can also be milked with a bottle to get pickle juice to drink or to craft the colorful potion. You can also boost them with orange peels(obtained from orange penguins, which spawn on top of sand in mesas) and chocolate catfish.
