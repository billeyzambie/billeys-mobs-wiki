Obtaining / Spawning

Rats appear underground in medium light levels.

Taming

They are tamed using poultry oranges, apples, melon or sweet berries.

Abilities

Robbing Monsters

Once tamed, they will attack and steal certain items from monsters, when you attack a mob that's holding an item rats can steal.

Rats will usually also have a chance to give the stolen item a random enchantment, and they can only steal the following items: bow, crossbow, iron axe, iron sword, iron shovel, stone sword, gold sword, gold axe and trident.

Cooking

If you equip one with a pet chef hat(crafted with kiwi feathers and wool) and give it a bowl, you'll get a bowl of ratatouille, which gives regeneration, resistance and night vision when eaten.

If you give it a glass bottle instead, you'll get a bottle of rat beer, which gives strength when drank but also has a chance to give nausea and/or mining fatigue.

Putting them on your head

Players can also put them on your head by petting them(sneak + right click/long tap) and then walking into them.

Rats will usually get off your head either when you jump while sneaking or when you hit a mob that's holding an item rats can steal. Also, while rats are generally on top of your head, they are immune to any kind of damage.

Breeding

Crossbreeding

Players can get color patterns by breeding two rats of a different color.
