Obtaining

The mercat plushie is obtained by completing the "Mercat Napper" advancement and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

Uses

It doesn't do anything on pets but it can also be placed down and worn by players as a helmet.

It is generally enchantable and has infinite durability but no protection.
