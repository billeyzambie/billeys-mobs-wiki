Obtaining

The pet hat is crafted with 4 kiwi feathers and 1 leather and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

If a pet wearing one receives damage that would've normally killed it, the hat will usually absorb the damage and get destroyed.
