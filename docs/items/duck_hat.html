Obtaining

The duck hat is a hat that looks like a duck and is obtained by killing the rare zombie that wears it and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

Uses

It constantly gives the pet wearing it the Strength I effect.

It also randomly quacks and plays the duck hurt and death sound when its wearer is generally damaged or dies.

It can also be worn by players as a helmet for the same effect.

It is generally enchantable and has infinite durability but no protection.
