Rubber Ducky

The rubber ducky is rarely found by ducks in water and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

  • Item ID: billey:rubber_ducky

Obtaining

The rubber ducky is rarely found by ducks in water and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

Uses

Makes the pet wearing it more likely to combo its target by dealing a bit of delayed damage and knockback.

Also causes pets that normally wouldn't defend you to defend you anyway.

On hit, the target is knocked back and takes a delayed extra hit after 10 ticks (0.5 seconds).

The delayed hit deals 3 damage and does not trigger if the pet is a slime wyvern.
