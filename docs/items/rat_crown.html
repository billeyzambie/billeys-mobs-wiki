Rat Crown

The rat crown is obtained by killing a Rat King and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

  • Item ID: billey:rat_crown

Obtaining

The rat crown is obtained by killing a Rat King and can be equipped on any Billey's Mobs pet or vanilla cat.

Uses

Makes the pet wearing it summon rat minions when it attacks.

Also causes pets that normally wouldn't defend you to defend you anyway.

The crown spawns up to four minions around the pet when it acquires a target, healing existing minions to full.

Duckatrices wearing the crown can spawn duck, pigeon, deinonychus, or yutyrannus minions based on level, with higher tiers requiring level 3/6/10.
